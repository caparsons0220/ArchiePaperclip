# Archie Skills

_Created: 2026-04-17_
_Companion to `ARCHIE_BRAVO.md`. Catalog of the expertise lenses Archie loads when running a business. Each skill is a **mindset / expertise / workflow**, not a task._

## What A Skill Is

A skill is the **lens Archie applies to work** — the mindset of an expert in that domain. Not a task list. Not steps to follow. The way a seasoned practitioner thinks.

Following Claude's skill format: the title names the expertise, and the body captures the lens — when to think this way, what to prioritize, what to avoid, what "done well" looks like. Archie loads relevant skills when he picks up work; one skill applies to hundreds of downstream tasks.

**Example:** The "Senior Product Designer" skill isn't "build a landing page." It's the mindset of a staff-level product designer. That mindset gets applied to microcopy, spacing, color, flows, accessibility, whatever the work is. The skill is the lens. Tasks are downstream.

## How Skills Work

- **Templates declare default loadouts.** Each template in `ARCHIE_BRAVO_TEMPLATE_EXAMPLES.md` ships with a default skill loadout — e.g., a Newsletter business loads Audience Obsession Machine + Content Flywheel Engineer + Algorithm Whisperer.
- **Agenda items can pull additional skills.** If a task calls for expertise outside the default loadout, Archie pulls that skill for that wake and drops it after.
- **Users create custom skills via prompt-to-skill.** User describes the expertise they want Archie to have ("think like a patent lawyer specializing in software IP"), Archie generates the full skill definition, user edits, saves.
- **Skills can be shared via the marketplace** (v2). Third-party experts publish domain skills. Creators earn revenue share. Platform takes a cut.

## Combined Duplicates

These skills appeared in multiple business categories in their source form. Combined here into single lenses that load across all the relevant templates:

- **Upsell & Expansion Artist** appeared in Agency, Freelance, and (as "Upsell & Add-On Artist") Personal Services. E-commerce has its own "Upsell & Bundle Genius" which is distinct enough (bundle mechanics) to keep separate. Agency / Freelance / Personal Services all load the single "Upsell & Expansion Artist" entry catalogued in Universal.
- **Referral & Testimonial handling** appeared as "Referral & Testimonial Natural" (Coaching), "Review & Social Proof Alchemist" (E-commerce), "Referral Engine Builder" + "Review & Testimonial Magnet" (Personal Services). Combined as two Universal skills: **Referral Engine Builder** and **Review & Testimonial Magnet**, which load across all relevant templates.

## Universal Skills (Load Across All Applicable Business Categories)

### Upsell & Expansion Artist
The mindset of someone who naturally spots opportunities to grow one-off work into bigger deals, retainers, and long-term expansions. Treats upsells as genuine improvements to the customer's life, not margin grabs. Reads usage, engagement, and timing signals to know when the moment is right to suggest more, and walks away when it isn't.

### Referral Engine Builder
The lens of creating natural, effortless referral loops without ever asking awkwardly. Builds relationships so strong that clients refer without being prompted, designs systems that make referring frictionless, and treats the happy customer's network as the most powerful acquisition channel.

### Review & Testimonial Magnet
The mindset of making customers want to rave about the experience publicly. Turns satisfied moments into captured social proof, makes the act of leaving a review feel like participation instead of a chore, and treats reviews as the most powerful marketing asset the business has.

## User-Created Skills (Prompt-to-Skill)

Users describe an expertise they want Archie to embody, Archie generates the full skill markdown. Example user prompts:

- "Think like my CPA who specializes in real estate investors"
- "Think like a patent attorney who specializes in software IP"
- "Think like a Michelin-trained chef running a ghost kitchen"
- "Think like a senior brand strategist at a luxury fashion house"
- "Think like a YC partner reviewing a pitch"

Archie produces the skill in the same format (name + mindset description), user reviews, edits, saves. Skill is then available in that user's Skills library and can be loaded into any North Star.

## Skill Marketplace (v2)

Third-party experts publish domain-specific skills. Buyers pay a subscription for access to premium skills. Platform takes revenue share. Creators earn passive income from expertise packaged as a prompt. Quality enforced via reviews + usage metrics.
